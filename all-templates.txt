# HOW TO SUBMIT TEMPLATES TO META
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: business.facebook.com
2. Navigate to: WhatsApp Manager → Message Templates
3. Click "Create Template"
4. Fill in:
   - Template name (use names above, e.g. gym_welcome_intake)
   - Category (UTILITY or MARKETING as noted)
   - Language: English
   - Copy-paste the body text above
   - Add variables {{1}}, {{2}} etc. in the right places
5. Submit for review (24–48 hours)
6. Once approved, use template name in your code

IMPORTANT RULES:
✅ UTILITY templates: transactional, order confirmations, reminders — free to send
⚠️ MARKETING templates: promotional content — charged per message (~₹0.35–0.58 each)
❌ Do NOT send marketing templates to users who haven't opted in
❌ Do NOT send messages with prohibited content (gambling, adult content)

For Indian businesses:
- Register on Meta Business Suite with Indian business docs
- Verify your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)
- Your phone number must be dedicated (not used for personal WA)
